Mr. Shahab Afshari is a Ph.D. candidate of Civil Engineering/Water Resources studying and experimenting the utilization and development of analytical and modeling skills in hydrological and riverine sciences. His career goals include: involvement in scientific research, interpretation, and statistical assessment of hydrological and riverine data; developing or improving the existing models; and exploring sensitivities of the model components to geophysical and environmental factors through local to regional scales.
